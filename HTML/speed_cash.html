RDB Speed Cash

RDB Speed Cash is a fast-track financial facility provided against the mortgage of a vehicle. It is specifically designed for applicants with a stable income to meet their vehicle-related financial goals or other urgent liquidity needs.

Whether you are looking to purchase a modern or reconditioned vehicle, or require immediate cash for personal emergencies using your registered vehicle as security, Speed Cash offers a rapid approval process.

100,000
Minimum Loan Amount
14%
Fixed Interest Rate
02 Years
Maximum Repayment
  • Eligible Activities: Purchase of modern/reconditioned vehicles or meeting urgent financial needs for registered vehicle owners.
  • Loan Amount: Minimum Rs. 100,000/-. Final amount is determined by the valuation of the vehicle and the repayment capacity of the applicant.
  • Interest Rate: Attractive rate of 14% p.a.
  • Age: Between 18 and 60 years. (Note: The loan must be fully repaid before reaching the age of 60).
  • Credit History: No CRIB issues or defaults.
  • Legal Status: No legal impediments or restrictions.
  • Income: Must possess a stable and revealable source of income.
  • Absolute ownership/Mortgage of the relevant vehicle.
  • Two (02) suitable personal securities (Guarantors).
