Consumption Loans

Consumption loans are one of the most popular personal loan options at RDB, designed to help you meet your immediate financial needs for any legally acceptable activity.

Whether it is for household requirements, education, or other personal milestones, our consumption loans provide you with the financial flexibility you need with competitive rates and a stress-free repayment process.

5 Mn
Maximum Loan Amount
13.5%
Interest Rates Onwards
10 Years
Maximum Repayment
  • Eligible Activities: Any consumption requirement that is legally acceptable.
  • Limitations: Applicants must have sufficient existing income that can be clearly revealed and verified.
  • Government/Statutory Board Employees: 13.5%
  • Other Employees: 15%
  • Detu Saviya: 15%
  • Be a citizen of Sri Lanka
  • Should not be a willful defaulter in any financial institution
  • Must demonstrate an effective repayment capacity
  • Permanent employment or stable income source required
  • 3 Recent Salary Slips
  • Identification: National Identity Card (NIC) / Passport
  • 2 Guarantors with acceptable income levels
  • Letter from employer confirming service (if applicable)
  • Utility bill as proof of residence
