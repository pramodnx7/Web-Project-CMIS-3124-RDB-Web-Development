  • Trust of a Government Bank: Security and stability of a state-owned institution.
  • Flexible Terms: Customized repayment terms and less paperwork.
  • Competitive Rates: Lowest monthly rentals and competitive interest rates.
  • Eligible Vehicles: Lorries, Freezer Trucks, Trucks, Cabs, Buses, Vans, Off-road vehicles, and Land vehicles.
  • Should be a registered enterprise, professional, fixed income earner, or high net worth individual.
  • Must open and operate a savings account with Regional Development Bank (RDB).
  • Should not be a defaulter of previous financial facilities from any lending institution.
  • Capacity to generate sufficient cash flow to pay lease rentals.
  • Owner/Partner must be a Sri Lankan citizen aged 18 to 60.
  • Prior experience or knowledge in the related enterprise.
  • Maximum Lease Amount: Rs. 50 Million.
  • Repayment: Maximum up to 7 years.
  • Collateral: Lease agreement, absolute ownership of the leased asset in the name of the bank, and other securities acceptable to the bank.
