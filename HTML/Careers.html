Careers at RDB

At Regional Development Bank, we believe in nurturing talent and fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and growth. As Sri Lanka's premier state-owned development bank, we offer rewarding career opportunities for passionate individuals who want to make a meaningful impact on the nation's economic development.

Our team of over 3,000 dedicated professionals works across 272 service points nationwide, serving more than 6 million customers. We are committed to creating an inclusive workplace that values diversity, promotes continuous learning, and encourages professional development at every level.

Join us in our mission to empower rural communities, support entrepreneurs, and drive sustainable economic growth across Sri Lanka. Whether you're starting your career or looking to advance to the next level, RDB offers dynamic opportunities in banking, finance, technology, customer service, and management.

Explore our current openings and discover how you can be part of a team that's dedicated to building a stronger, more prosperous Sri Lanka.

Banking Operations

Join our front-line teams to deliver exceptional banking services to communities across Sri Lanka.

Finance & Accounting

Drive financial excellence and strategic planning to support our mission of inclusive development.

Customer Service

ASSISTANT MANAGER

- INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

You should ideally:

  • possess a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field from a recognized university
  • hold professional certifications such as AWS, Azure, CCNA or equivalent will be an added qualification
  • have a minimum of 4 to 6 years experience in IT infrastructure, software development or system administration
  • possess strong knowledge in database management, network security and cloud technologies
  • have experience with core banking systems or financial software applications
  • possess excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in a team environment
  • have good communication skills in English and Sinhala

You will be responsible for:

  • managing and maintaining the bank's IT infrastructure and systems
  • implementing and overseeing cybersecurity measures and protocols
  • coordinating with vendors for system upgrades and maintenance
  • providing technical support and training to staff members
  • developing and implementing disaster recovery and business continuity plans
  • ensuring compliance with IT policies and regulatory requirements

A competitive remuneration package and other fringe benefits as well as structured career advancement opportunities and extensive training are on offer for the chosen candidate.

We are an equal opportunity employer, committed to promoting an inclusive and diverse environment. Recruitment to the Bank is based solely on merit and competency. Any form of canvassing is discouraged.

Correspondence will only be with the short-listed candidates.

Please apply via e-mail by sending an updated CV or a RDB Bank application form which could be downloaded from our website to recruit@rdb.lk with the position applied for mentioned in the subject by 28th February 2026.

CREDIT OFFICER

- CREDIT & LENDING

You should ideally:

  • possess a Bachelor's Degree in Banking, Finance, Economics or Business Administration
  • have professional qualifications from IBSL, CIMA or ACCA will be an advantage
  • have a minimum of 2 to 4 years experience in credit evaluation, loan processing or related banking operations
  • possess strong analytical skills and understanding of credit risk assessment
  • have knowledge of agricultural financing and rural development lending
  • possess excellent interpersonal skills and ability to engage with diverse customer segments
  • be fluent in Sinhala and/or Tamil with working knowledge of English

You will be responsible for:

  • evaluating loan applications and conducting credit assessments
  • preparing credit proposals and recommendations for approval committees
  • monitoring loan portfolios and ensuring timely recovery
  • conducting site visits and verification of collateral
  • providing guidance to customers on loan products and repayment terms
  • maintaining accurate documentation and records

EXECUTIVE

- FINANCE & ACCOUNTING

You should ideally:

  • possess a Bachelor's Degree in Accounting, Finance or Commerce
  • be a partly qualified or fully qualified member of CIMA, ACCA, CA Sri Lanka or equivalent
  • have a minimum of 2 to 3 years experience in accounting, financial reporting or auditing
  • possess strong knowledge of SLFRS/IFRS standards
  • have proficiency in accounting software and MS Excel
  • be detail-oriented with excellent organizational skills
