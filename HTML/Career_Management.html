Management & Leadership

Strategic leadership driving organizational excellence

About This Department

Our Management team shapes the strategic direction of RDB, ensures operational excellence across 272 branches, and drives the bank's mission of regional development. Leaders at RDB are responsible for guiding teams, making critical decisions, and fostering a culture of innovation and service.

Join the team that's leading one of Sri Lanka's most important development institutions and making financial inclusion a reality.

Why Join Management?

Strategic Impact

Shape the future of development banking in Sri Lanka

Lead Large Teams

Manage and develop teams across multiple branches

Top-tier Benefits

Executive compensation packages with retirement benefits

National Recognition

Be part of a nationally recognized banking institution

Available Roles

General Manager / CEO

Executive Level

Lead the entire organization, execute board directives, and drive strategic growth initiatives.

Key Requirements:
  • 20+ years banking experience
  • MBA or equivalent qualification
  • Proven C-suite experience

Deputy General Manager

Executive Level

Support the GM/CEO in strategic planning and oversee major functional areas of the bank.

Key Requirements:
  • 15+ years banking experience
  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Senior management experience

Regional Manager

Senior Level

Oversee banking operations across multiple branches within a designated region.

Key Requirements:
  • 12+ years banking experience
  • Branch management background
  • Strong leadership abilities

Senior Manager

Senior Level

Lead specialized departments such as Credit, Operations, Risk, or Business Development.

Key Requirements:
  • 10+ years relevant experience
  • Domain expertise
  • Team management skills

General Requirements

Advanced degree (MBA, Master's) strongly preferred
Proven track record in banking leadership
Strategic thinking and decision-making abilities
Understanding of regulatory requirements and compliance
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
