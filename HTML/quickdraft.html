Quick Draft Loan

Quick Draft Loan Scheme is designed to meet the short-term funding needs of small, medium and large enterprises as well as self-employed individuals.

The facility supports investment improvements, emergency working capital needs, and revolving working capital requirements to ensure smooth business operations.

21%
Interest Rate
2 Guarantors
Loan Security
Up to 4 Years
Max Repayment
  • Purpose: Meet short-term funding needs of enterprises and self-employed.
  • Eligibility:
    • Be a citizen of Sri Lanka
    • Should not be a willful defaulter
    • Have effective repayment capacity
  • Loan Security: Two guarantors
  • Income Requirement: Sufficient existing income must be proven.
