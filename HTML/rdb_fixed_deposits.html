RDB Fixed Deposits

RDB fixed deposits are identified as a popular mode of investments among its individual and corporate customers. FDs are available at RDB for periods of 1, 2, 3 and 6 months as well as 1-5 years. Since the inception, the Bank was able to provide very attractive interest rates compared to other banks. Special interest rates are offered for fixed deposits with maturity periods of 1 year and above.
