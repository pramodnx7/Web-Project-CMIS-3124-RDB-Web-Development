Finance & Accounting

Financial management and reporting excellence

About This Department

The Finance & Accounting department ensures financial integrity, regulatory compliance, and strategic financial planning at RDB. We are responsible for managing the bank's financial resources, preparing financial statements, budgeting, and ensuring compliance with Central Bank regulations and international accounting standards.

Join us to drive financial excellence at one of Sri Lanka's leading development banks and contribute to sustainable growth and transparency.

Why Join Finance & Accounting?

Financial Impact

Drive financial strategy for a leading development bank

Professional Growth

Support for professional certifications (CIMA, ACCA, CA)

Modern Systems

Work with advanced financial systems and reporting tools

Job Security

Stable career with government banking benefits

Available Roles

Chief Financial Officer

Executive

Provide strategic financial leadership, oversee financial planning and analysis, and ensure sound financial management across the organization.

Key Requirements:
  • 15+ years financial management experience
  • Fully qualified CA/CIMA/ACCA
  • MBA or Masters in Finance preferred

Finance Manager

Senior Level

Oversee financial operations, manage the finance team, prepare budgets, and ensure timely and accurate financial reporting.

Key Requirements:
  • 8+ years experience in finance
  • Professional qualification (CIMA, ACCA, CA)
  • Strong leadership skills

Accountant

Mid Level

Manage financial transactions, reconcile accounts, prepare financial statements, and ensure compliance with accounting standards.

Key Requirements:
  • 3-5 years accounting experience
  • Partly qualified CA/CIMA/ACCA
  • Knowledge of SLFRS/IFRS

Finance Executive

Entry Level

Support finance operations, process accounting entries, assist with financial reporting, and maintain accurate financial records.

Key Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting/Finance
  • Pursuing professional qualification
  • Proficiency in MS Excel

General Requirements

Professional qualifications (CIMA, ACCA, CA Sri Lanka) or pursuing
Strong knowledge of SLFRS/IFRS standards
Proficiency in accounting software and MS Excel
Attention to detail and strong analytical skills
Good communication skills in English
