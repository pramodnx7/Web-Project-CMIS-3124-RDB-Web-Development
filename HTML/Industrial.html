Industrial Development Loan

RDB's Industrial Development Loan is a strategic financial facility designed to empower Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) across Sri Lanka. We provide the capital needed to drive industrial innovation and growth.

Whether you are expanding your production line or modernizing your facility, our loan offers competitive rates and flexible terms tailored to your specific project requirements.

12% - 15%
Competitive Interest Rate
Project Based
Flexible Loan Amount
7 Years
Max Repayment Period
  • Purpose: Promote and develop Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs).
  • Eligibility:
    • Must be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
    • Should not be a willful defaulter.
    • Must have an effective repayment capacity.
  • Area of Operation: All island (Available at all RDB branches).
  • Fixed Rate: 15%
  • Variable Rate: 12%

* Rates are subject to project evaluation and bank authority guidelines.

  • Loan Amount: Depending on the specific requirements and feasibility of the project.
  • Maximum Repayment Period: 7 Years.
  • Accepted Securities:
    • Personal guarantors
    • Movable assets
    • Immovable assets

For more information, please contact the Manager at your nearest RDB Branch to discuss your industrial project.
