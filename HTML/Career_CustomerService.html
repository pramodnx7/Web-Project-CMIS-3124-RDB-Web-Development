Customer Service

Excellence in customer experience and support

About This Department

Our Customer Service team is the heart of RDB's commitment to providing exceptional banking experiences. We handle customer inquiries, resolve issues, and ensure every interaction leaves our customers satisfied and valued.

With over 272 branches serving rural and urban communities, you'll be part of a team that makes a real difference in people's lives every day.

Why Join Customer Service?

People First Culture

Work in an environment that values relationships and teamwork

Communication Skills

Develop exceptional interpersonal and communication abilities

Island-wide Impact

Serve customers across all 25 districts of Sri Lanka

Comprehensive Training

Extensive training programs on products and service excellence

Available Roles

Customer Service Manager

Senior Level

Lead customer service teams, develop service strategies, and ensure customer satisfaction across branches.

Key Requirements:
  • 8+ years customer service experience
  • Team leadership experience
  • Strong analytical skills

Call Center Supervisor

Mid Level

Supervise call center operations, monitor quality, and coach team members to achieve service targets.

Key Requirements:
  • 5+ years call center experience
  • Quality monitoring expertise
  • Team management skills

Customer Relations Officer

Mid Level

Handle customer inquiries, resolve complaints, and build lasting relationships with bank customers.

Key Requirements:
  • 3+ years banking experience
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Bilingual (Sinhala/Tamil/English)

Call Center Agent

Entry Level

Handle customer calls, provide product information, and assist with basic banking inquiries.

Key Requirements:
  • Degree or Diploma holder
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Computer literacy

General Requirements

Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Fluency in Sinhala and/or Tamil, with English proficiency
Customer-first mindset with patience and empathy
Ability to work in shifts when required
