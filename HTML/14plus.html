14 Plus

RDB 14 Plus is a specially designed savings account for young adults aged 14 to 18 years. This account bridges the gap between childhood savings and adult banking, providing teenagers with the tools and knowledge to manage their finances independently while still under parental guidance.

With attractive interest rates, free debit card, and online banking access, 14 Plus empowers young Sri Lankans to develop financial literacy and independence, preparing them for their future financial journey.
