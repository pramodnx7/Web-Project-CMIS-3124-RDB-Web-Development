Information Technology

Digital innovation and technology solutions

About This Department

Our IT team drives digital transformation at RDB, developing innovative banking solutions and maintaining robust infrastructure. We are responsible for core banking systems, digital payment platforms, cybersecurity, network infrastructure, and innovative fintech solutions.

Be part of the team that's bringing modern banking to rural Sri Lanka and transforming how millions access financial services.

Why Join Information Technology?

Cutting-Edge Tech

Work with modern banking systems and emerging technologies

Cybersecurity Focus

Protect critical financial infrastructure and customer data

Innovation Projects

Lead digital banking initiatives and fintech innovations

Certifications Support

Training and support for AWS, Azure, CCNA certifications

Available Roles

IT Manager

Senior Level

Lead IT strategy, manage infrastructure, and oversee technology projects across the organization.

Key Requirements:
  • 10+ years IT experience
  • Experience with banking systems
  • AWS/Azure certifications preferred

Software Developer

Mid Level

Design, develop, and maintain banking applications and internal systems using modern technologies.

Key Requirements:
  • 3-5 years development experience
  • Java, .NET, or Python proficiency
  • Database management skills

Network Administrator

Mid Level

Manage network infrastructure, ensure connectivity across 272 branches, and maintain security protocols.

Key Requirements:
  • CCNA/CCNP certification
  • Network security experience
  • 3+ years in networking

IT Support Officer

Entry Level

Provide technical support to staff, troubleshoot hardware/software issues, and maintain IT equipment.

Key Requirements:
  • Degree in IT/Computer Science
  • Hardware troubleshooting skills
  • Good communication abilities

General Requirements

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT or related field
Experience with core banking systems is an advantage
Knowledge of cybersecurity best practices
Professional certifications (AWS, Azure, CCNA) preferred
