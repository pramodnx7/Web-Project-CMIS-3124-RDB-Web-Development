Human Resources

Building and nurturing our greatest asset - our people

About This Department

Our Human Resources team is responsible for attracting, developing, and retaining top talent across RDB's extensive branch network. We manage recruitment, employee development, performance management, compensation & benefits, and workplace culture initiatives.

With over 4,000 employees across the island, our HR team plays a vital role in ensuring RDB remains an employer of choice in the banking sector.

Why Join Human Resources?

People-Centric Work

Make a direct impact on employee lives and careers

Professional Growth

Opportunity for CIPM, SHRM certifications

Large Workforce

Manage HR for 4,000+ employees island-wide

Shape Policy

Influence HR policies and organizational culture

Available Roles

HR Manager

Senior Level

Lead HR strategy, manage employee relations, and oversee all HR functions across the organization.

Key Requirements:
  • 10+ years HR experience
  • CIPM/SHRM certification
  • Knowledge of Sri Lankan labor laws

Training & Development Officer

Mid Level

Design and deliver training programs to enhance employee skills and organizational capabilities.

Key Requirements:
  • 5+ years L&D experience
  • Training facilitation skills
  • Curriculum development expertise

Recruitment Officer

Mid Level

Manage end-to-end recruitment processes, talent acquisition, and employer branding initiatives.

Key Requirements:
  • 3+ years recruitment experience
  • Interview and assessment skills
  • Sourcing and networking abilities

HR Executive

Entry Level

Support HR operations including payroll processing, leave management, and employee documentation.

Key Requirements:
  • Degree in HRM or related field
  • HRIS system knowledge
  • Attention to detail

General Requirements

Bachelor's degree in Human Resource Management, Business Administration, or related field
CIPM, SHRM, or equivalent professional certification is an advantage
Knowledge of Sri Lankan labor laws and employment regulations
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
Proficiency in HRIS and MS Office applications
