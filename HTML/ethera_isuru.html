Foreign Employment Loan

Ethera Isuru loans are identified as a popular mode of personal loans specifically designed to facilitate Sri Lankan citizens who wish to engage in foreign employment.

This facility aims to ease the financial burden of migration, providing the necessary support for travel, documentation, and initial settlement costs for those seeking a better future abroad.

1 Mn
Maximum Loan Amount
18%
Interest Rate (Bank Fund)
03 Years
Maximum Repayment
  • Purpose: To facilitate Sri Lankan citizens who wish to engage in foreign employment.
  • Limitations: Applicants should have sufficient income, and it could be revealed/verified by the bank.
  • Permanent employees of public service, corporations, statutory bodies, semi-government or state-owned enterprises.
  • Private sector employees or employees without a fixed income (subject to verification).
  • Must have obtained approval from the respective firm where currently employed.
  • Must fulfill all eligibility criteria of the Sri Lanka Foreign Employment Bureau (SLBFE).
  • Must be willing to engage in foreign employment with a valid visa and passport.
  • Age: Between 18 and 50 years.
  • Should not have any non-performing loan (NPL) obligations.
  • Application must include a joint borrower from the family (Aged 18-60 years).
  • Approval letter obtained from the respective firm for foreign employment.
  • A power of attorney obtained by vesting power to a person to deal with the bank on behalf of the applicant while abroad.
  • Valid Passport and Visa copies.
  • Proof of income/salary slips where applicable.
