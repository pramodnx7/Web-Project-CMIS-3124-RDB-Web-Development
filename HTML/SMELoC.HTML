SMELoC Loan

The SMELoC Loan scheme is designed to support Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) by providing accessible and affordable financing solutions.

This facility enables entrepreneurs to expand operations, purchase machinery, strengthen working capital, and improve productivity while benefiting from flexible repayment options.

10%
Interest Rate
Rs. 50 Mn
Maximum Loan
Flexible
Repayment Period
  • Purpose: Business expansion, machinery purchase, working capital.
  • Eligibility: Registered MSMEs & entrepreneurs.
  • Area: All island.
  • Security: Acceptable collateral as per bank guidelines.
  • Starting from 10% per annum
  • Concessionary rates available for priority sectors
  • Special terms for women & youth entrepreneurs
  • Financing up to Rs. 50 million
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Grace period available
  • Supports capital & working investments
  • Encourages local enterprise development
