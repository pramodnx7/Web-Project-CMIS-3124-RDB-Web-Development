Banking Operations

Front-line banking services and branch operations

About This Department

Our Banking Operations team is the heart of RDB, serving over 6 million customers through 272 service points across Sri Lanka. As part of this team, you'll directly contribute to our mission of uplifting rural communities and supporting grassroots development.

We provide essential banking services including savings accounts, deposits, withdrawals, fund transfers, and various financial transactions that keep communities economically active and thriving.

Why Join Banking Operations?

Community Impact

Make a real difference in rural communities across Sri Lanka

Career Growth

Clear promotion paths from entry-level to branch management

Comprehensive Training

Extensive banking and customer service training programs

Competitive Benefits

Attractive salary packages and government banking benefits

Available Roles

Branch Manager

Senior Level

Lead branch operations, manage staff, and drive business growth while ensuring excellent customer service and regulatory compliance.

Key Requirements:
  • 8+ years banking experience
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field

Assistant Branch Manager

Mid Level

Support branch operations, supervise staff, and ensure smooth daily operations while maintaining service quality standards.

Key Requirements:
  • 5+ years banking experience
  • Supervisory experience
  • Bachelor's degree preferred

Banking Assistant

Entry Level

Handle daily banking transactions, assist customers with inquiries, and support branch operations.

Key Requirements:
  • A/L qualification or above
  • Good communication skills
  • Computer literacy

Teller/Cashier

Entry Level

Process deposits, withdrawals, and payments while maintaining accurate records and ensuring cash security.

Key Requirements:
  • A/L qualification
  • Numerical accuracy
  • Customer service orientation

General Requirements

Bachelor's degree in Banking, Finance, Commerce or related field
Strong customer service orientation and interpersonal skills
Fluency in Sinhala and/or Tamil with working knowledge of English
Willingness to work at any branch location across Sri Lanka
Computer literacy and familiarity with banking software
