RDB Home Loans

RDB Bank Home Loans are designed to help you build, purchase, or renovate your dream home with speed and flexibility. We understand that your home is your greatest investment, and our tailor-made solutions ensure your journey to homeownership is smooth and affordable.

Whether you're looking to purchase a new property or renovate your existing residence, our competitive interest rates and long repayment periods make us the ideal partner for your housing needs.

50.0 Mn
Maximum Loan Amount
20 Years
Maximum Repayment
Rs. 40,000
Min. Monthly Income
  • Purchasing a house, apartment, or a plot of land for residential purposes.
  • Building a new house or completing a partially built house.
  • Renovating, extending, or completing an existing house.
  • Paying off an existing home-related loan from another recognized financial institution.

Standard Rates: Competitive market-based rates apply.

  • 0.50% Reduction: For Professionals and employees with a monthly salary above Rs. 200,000/-.
  • 0.25% Reduction: For Achiever, Vibe, Flash, and Anagi account holders.
  • Salaried employees, Professionals, or Entrepreneurs/Self-employed individuals with a proven track record.
  • Must be a Sri Lankan citizen (Foreign currency loans available for Sri Lankans employed abroad).
  • Minimum monthly net income of Rs. 40,000/- (Individual or Joint).
  • Joint applications are permitted with a spouse.

Personal: Loan application, Proof of professional membership (if applicable).

Income: Letter from employer, 3 months' salary slips, and 6 months of bank statements.

Property: Title deed, approved Survey Plan, approved Building Plan, Bill of Quantities (BOQ) for construction, and Local Authority documents (Street Line, Non-Vesting, etc.).
