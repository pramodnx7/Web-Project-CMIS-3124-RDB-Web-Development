RDB Detusavi Loan

RDB Detusavi is a specialized financial solution exclusively designed for pensioners. We understand your lifelong service to the nation and offer this facility to help you fulfill your post-retirement aspirations with dignity.

Whether it's for household improvements, a special family occasion, or personal consumption needs, Detusavi provides easy access to credit with high repayment periods and competitive interest rates.

10 Years
Max Repayment
14%
Lowest Interest Rate
70 Yrs
Age Limit
  • Purpose: All legally acceptable consumption purposes.
  • Loan Securities: Secured via Loan Protection Insurance.
  • Loan Amount: Based on the pension salary capacity.
  • Operation Area: Available island-wide.

Repayment Period below 5 years: 14% p.a.

Repayment Period above 5 years: 15% p.a.

Maximum Repayment Period: Up to 10 Years

  • Be a citizen of Sri Lanka.
  • Must be a pensioner.
  • Should not be a willful defaulter in any financial institution.
  • Demonstrate effective repayment capacity.
  • Maximum age at the end of the loan period should be below 70 years.
